Description

This web application was created in order to be able to manage sports competitions, in which they can register teams, players, competitions and matches. In addition, you can view the table of positions of each competition and the results of the matches.

The application was developed with the Django framework, which is a framework that is based on the model-view-template architecture, which allows the separation of the logic of the application from the presentation of the data. In addition, it has a database in which the data of the teams, players, competitions and matches are stored.

Features

Create your own sporting event

Imagine the excitement of designing and hosting your own soccer tournament, basketball competition, track meet, or any other sporting event you love. From the choice of location and dates, to the selection of teams or participants, every detail is in your hands.

Our application offers you an intuitive and easy-to-use interface that will guide you through each step of the process. You can customize the rules, set the prizes and trophies, invite your friends and family, and watch the action unfold in real time through live updates and real-time statistics.

Times and dates for competitions

You can also choose the location, dates, and times of your event. Whether you want to host a tournament in your own backyard or in a professional stadium, our app gives you the flexibility to choose the perfect venue.

Wide range of sports for competitions

Explore a wide range of sports and activities available for your events. Whether you prefer soccer, basketball, volleyball, or even more unique activities like obstacle courses, you can customize each event to suit your sports interests and passions.
